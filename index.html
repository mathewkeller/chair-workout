High-Knee Power March

Sit tall and march in place, lifting your knees high and swinging your arms with control and power. Keep your core tight throughout.

Mobility Tip: Lift one leg at a time with both hands on your thighs for support. March slower, or tap toes in place if raising legs is uncomfortable.

Seated Power Punches (Cross & Hook)

Sit up tall and punch forward across your body (cross) then use a rounded hook punch to the side. Engage your core as you twist.

Mobility Tip: Keep arms lower and punch slowly in front of the body. Avoid twisting if it causes discomfort in the joints.

Leg Extensions with Hold

Extend one leg straight out and hold for 5 seconds, then switch legs. Engage your thigh and core muscles as you lift.

Mobility Tip: Instead of holding, gently extend and lower one leg at a time without locking the knee. Keep the movement short and supported.

Chair Plank Taps

Lean slightly back while seated, feet off the ground, and tap your knees or shins alternately with both hands. Keep your abs tight and controlled.

Mobility Tip: Keep both feet on the ground and gently reach across the knees instead of lifting legs. Maintain upright posture for stability.

Seated Bicycle Crunch

Bring your right elbow toward your left knee, then alternate. Mimic a pedaling motion while rotating your torso to target your obliques.

Mobility Tip: Do small torso turns without bringing knees up. Use gentle arm movements instead of twisting if sensitive to rotation.

Chair Squat Hover

Rise a few inches off the chair and hover, then sit back down with control. Keep your back straight and feet grounded.

Mobility Tip: Simply shift weight slightly forward and back while staying seated. Alternatively, press into the feet gently without rising.

Breathing Stretch + Flex

Inhale deeply and raise your arms overhead. Exhale while lowering your arms and gently twisting side to side. Focus on controlled breathing.

Mobility Tip: Keep both hands on thighs and focus on deep breathing with a slight shoulder roll. Keep movements small and smooth.

Chair Push & Pull

While seated, push your arms forward forcefully, then pull your elbows back as if rowing. Keep your chest lifted and squeeze your shoulder blades on the pull.

Mobility Tip: Perform smaller push and pull movements close to the chest. Keep shoulders relaxed and avoid jerking motions.

Chair Torso Twists with Reach

Sit tall and twist your torso to reach your opposite hand toward the outside of your thigh. Alternate sides with each rep to work your obliques.

Mobility Tip: Gently twist just the head or shoulders. Use a small range of motion and support hands on thighs if needed.

Single-Leg Seated Hold

Extend one leg straight out and hold for 15 seconds, keeping your back upright and core tight. Switch legs.

Mobility Tip: Keep toes touching the ground and just slide one heel forward. Hold only for a breath or two if full extension is too much.

Shadow Boxing Combo

Perform a fast-paced combo of jab, cross, hook, and uppercut punches while seated. Engage your abs and keep your arms moving continuously.

Mobility Tip: Use slow, controlled arm movements. Keep arms below shoulder height and avoid full twisting if that causes strain.

Calf Raises

While seated, lift your heels off the ground so you're on the balls of your feet, then slowly lower back down. Repeat in a controlled motion to work your calf muscles.

Mobility Tip: Keep feet flat and press gently into the floor without lifting heels. Focus on small movements or just engage the muscles without moving.

Gentle Twists and Holds

Sit comfortably and gently twist your torso to one side, holding for a few seconds. Return to centre and repeat on the other side. Keep the motion smooth and within your comfort range.

Mobility Tip: You can simply look to the left or right without moving your body. Hands can rest on thighs for stability.
